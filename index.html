Features

  • Color-coded stickers for each item
  • Infrared detection via mobile camera
  • Statistics based on how often you lose items
  • Network of locators if your object is lost and out of range

How it Works

Attach Your dtectors

Place your color-coded dtector sticker on your item (keys, phone, laptop, wallet) and register that item to your application.

Start Searching

Begin the search for your lost item by clicking on the item and then pressing "Find." You will then be presented with a compass and a distance that will lead you to your lost item.

Item Retrieval

As you approach your item, the indicator sound will begin to beep faster until you are within 5 inches of your item. Once located, click the "Found" button on the application/
